# Evil Universe MAD Farming Plan

This plan outlines the roadmap to transition from the Standard Universe to the Evil Universe, build up a solid Mastery multiplier, gather CRISPR upgrades, and prepare the necessary traits for your first custom race (the Ancients).

## Phase 1: Transition to Evil (Current Step)

1. **Complete the Dryad Run**: Finish your current Dryad run in the Standard universe.
2. **Perform a Black Hole Reset**: Trigger the Black Hole reset. 
3. **Select Your Destination**: Choose the **Evil Universe** as your target.
4. **Planet Selection**: You will be offered exactly **4 planet choices** (fixed for Black Hole resets). Select the best biome using the following priority:
   - **Oceanic or Swamp** (Aquatic genus: Sharkin / Octigoran)
   - **Volcanic or Ashland** (Heat genus: Phoenix / Salamander)
   - **Tundra or Taiga** (Polar genus: Yeti / Wendigo)
   - **Desert** (Sand genus: Tuskin / Kamel)
   - **Grassland or Forest** (Standard fallback)

---

## Phase 2: Set Up the Challenge Environment

1. **Unlock Hardened Genes**: In your very first run in the Evil universe, go to the **ARPA -> CRISPR** tab and purchase **Hardened Genes** for **5 Plasmids**. This unlocks challenge toggles for subsequent runs.
2. **Challenge Level Recommendation**: Run at **2★ difficulty** using:
   - **No Free Trade** (`no_trade`): Disables trading with other nations. Easy to manage by allocating extra citizens to basic gathering.
   - **Weak CRISPR Discounts** (`no_crispr`): Disables CRISPR upgrades.
   - **DO NOT enable No Manual Crafting (`no_craft`)**: Your auto-buy script relies heavily on manual crafting clicks (`resEl.__vue__.craft()`) to bypass low craftsmen speeds. Keeping manual crafting enabled keeps your runs fast.
   - **DO NOT enable No Plasmids (`no_plasmid`)**: You need the passive production bonus from plasmids since your base mastery is low.

---

## Phase 3: The MAD Grind (Target: 20–30 Resets)

Your goal is to complete **20 to 30 unique MAD resets** in the Evil Universe. Each run should be as fast as possible.

### General Strategy
*   **No Repeats**: Always choose a race you haven't reset in the Evil universe yet. Each unique race reset yields a new **Extinction Achievement** (standard + evil counterpart "Evil Twin" bonus).
*   **Target Genuses for Custom Race Traits**: Ensure you do a Bioseed (T2) reset on at least one race from each key genus to unlock all their traits in the Genelab:
    *   **Humanoid** (Human/Elf): Unlocks `creative` (reduced ARPA cost) and `studious` (research speed).
    *   **Plant** (Entish): Unlocks `kindling_kindred` (removes lumber).
    *   **Reptilian** (Gecko): Unlocks `chameleon` (critical Hell combat defense).
    *   **Small** (Gnome/Goblin): Unlocks `smart` and downrankable negative traits for genome points.

### MAD Run Setup
*   Focus strictly on reaching the 120k Knowledge cap and researching the MAD technology.
*   Do not overbuild production or space structures.
*   Keep a reserve of at least **250 Plasmids** unspent in your inventory at all times to maintain the maximum passive production speed bonus.

---

## Target Milestones before T3/T4 Progression

Before attempting another Black Hole (T3) or Ascension (T4) reset, aim for the following milestones:
*   **Mastery Multiplier**: $\ge 30\% - 45\%$ (up from 5.75%).
*   **Plasmid Buffer**: $\ge 1,000+$ unspent Plasmids.
*   **CRISPR Upgrades**: Purchase *Artificer* (crafting automation), *Governor* upgrades (for auto-management), and *Cost Creep* reductions.
*   **Genelab Unlocks**: Unlocked the traits for **Humanoid**, **Plant**, **Reptilian**, and **Small** genuses.
